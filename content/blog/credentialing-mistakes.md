## Credentialing Delays Are Revenue Delays

When payer enrollment runs behind schedule, the provider may be seeing patients without the ability to bill correctly. That creates avoidable write-offs and patient frustration.

## Start With a Complete Intake Packet

Collect licenses, board certifications, malpractice details, CAQH information, and work history before submission. Missing data almost always creates rework.

## Monitor Application Aging

Credentialing teams should maintain a live tracker that shows payer submission dates, follow-up activity, and expected effective dates.

### Escalate Before Deadlines Slip

If a payer application stalls, escalate before the intended go-live date. Late escalation is one of the main reasons new providers miss early revenue targets.

## Coordinate With Scheduling

Do not advertise a provider broadly until enrollment timing is clear. Scheduling patients too early can create out-of-network billing problems.
