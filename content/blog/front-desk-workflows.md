## Front-End Accuracy Drives Back-End Revenue

The front desk sets the quality of the data that billing teams inherit. Even strong coders and billers struggle when demographics or insurance details are wrong.

## Standardize Registration Questions

Every staff member should confirm the same patient fields in the same order. Consistency reduces missed data and makes training easier.

## Train Teams on Eligibility Red Flags

Registration staff should know how to spot inactive plans, coordination-of-benefits issues, and referral requirements before the visit starts.

### Use Scripts for Financial Conversations

Patients respond better when staff use clear, repeatable language for copays, deductibles, and self-pay expectations.

## Close the Loop With Billing

Weekly feedback from billing to front office helps teams understand which intake errors are causing denials and rework.
